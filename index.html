Professional Summary

Dr. Dionisio S. Bucao is a distinguished agricultural scientist and research leader at Mariano Marcos State University, making history as the first full-time researcher to be appointed as Research Director. With over 25 years of research experience and expertise in soil science, climate-resilient agriculture, and innovation management, he has been instrumental in advancing agricultural productivity and technology transfer in the Ilocos Region and beyond.

His work spans soil survey and classification, climate-resilient agriculture, garlic and agri-food condiments production, crop productivity enhancement, and Geographic Information Systems (GIS) applications. He has successfully led major research programs with total funding exceeding ‚Ç±350 million and has been recognized with multiple national and regional awards for excellence in research and innovation.
